Put two screenshots together

view_column Start Now

Sometimes you just need to put two screenshots next to each other. Maybe you want to show a before and after, compare two versions, or simply combine two images into one. It sounds simple, but most tools make it harder than it needs to be.

image add image

Why this is harder than it should be

If you try to combine two screenshots in a photo app or presentation software, you usually have to resize them manually, drag them into position, and hope they line up. The spacing is never quite right, and one image often ends up bigger than the other. For something so simple, it takes too many steps.

A simpler way

This tool lets you put two screenshots together without any manual adjustments. You choose whether you want them side by side or stacked vertically, upload your two images, and the layout handles the rest. Both screenshots stay the same size, properly aligned, with consistent spacing.

What you can do

With a two-image layout, you can:

  • Place two screenshots side by side horizontally
  • Stack two screenshots vertically
  • Keep both images at the same size
  • Get a clean result without manual alignment

When this is useful

This is useful when you need to:

  • Show a quick before and after
  • Compare two app screens or websites
  • Combine two related screenshots for sharing
  • Create a simple visual comparison

How it works

  1. Choose side by side or vertical layout
  2. Upload your two screenshots
  3. Export the combined image

The result is one clean image with both screenshots properly arranged. No resizing, no dragging, no guessing. Just two images put together the way you wanted.
