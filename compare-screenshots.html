Compare screenshots side by side

compare Start Comparing

When you need to compare screenshots, placing them clearly side by side is often harder than it sounds. Dragging images into a design tool usually leads to uneven spacing, misalignment, or unnecessary resizing. For UI comparison, before-and-after visuals, or feature differences, clarity matters more than decoration.

compare

The problem with general tools

Many people try to compare screenshots using general image editors or presentation tools. These tools are flexible, but they are not built for screenshot comparison. You often spend more time adjusting alignment than actually comparing content. The result may look fine, but it rarely feels clean or intentional.

A layout-first approach

This tool focuses on layout first. Instead of manually arranging images, you choose a comparison layout and place your screenshots into it. The layout defines alignment, spacing, and balance automatically, so every screenshot stays centered and visually consistent.

What you can do

With a layout-based approach, you can:

  • Compare two screenshots side by side
  • Place multiple screenshots in a structured grid
  • Create before-and-after comparisons
  • Keep spacing and alignment consistent without manual adjustment

When this is useful

This is useful when you need to:

  • Compare app interfaces or UI states
  • Show design changes or feature differences
  • Prepare screenshots for presentations or documentation
  • Share clear visual comparisons online

How it works

  1. Choose a comparison layout
  2. Upload your screenshots
  3. Export a clean, ready-to-use image

The result is a clean, balanced screenshot comparison that looks intentional and easy to read. No design setup, no manual alignment, and no unnecessary tools. Just structured layouts for clear visual comparison.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I compare two screenshots side by side?

Upload your screenshots to PictriKit, select the Compare layout, adjust spacing if needed, and export your comparison image. The entire process takes less than a minute.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, PictriKit is completely free. No registration or payment required.

Are my screenshots uploaded to a server?

No, all image processing happens locally in your browser. Your screenshots never leave your device.
