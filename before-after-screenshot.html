Before and after comparisons are one of the clearest ways to show change. Whether you're demonstrating a design update, a bug fix, or a feature improvement, placing two screenshots side by side makes the difference immediately visible.

Before
After

Why before and after works

When you describe a change in words, people have to imagine it. When you show it visually, they see it instantly. A before and after comparison removes ambiguity. The viewer's eye naturally moves from one image to the other, and the change becomes obvious without explanation.

The problem with manual comparisons

Creating a before and after image manually usually means opening both screenshots, resizing them to match, placing them next to each other, and hoping the alignment looks right. It's tedious work for something that should be simple. And if the images are slightly different sizes, the comparison looks unbalanced.

A layout that handles it for you

With a dedicated comparison layout, you upload your before screenshot and your after screenshot. The layout places them side by side with equal sizing and consistent spacing. The result looks intentional and professional, without any manual adjustment.

What you can do

With a before and after layout, you can:

  • Place two screenshots in a balanced side-by-side view
  • Show design changes or UI updates clearly
  • Compare old and new versions of any interface
  • Create comparison images for documentation or sharing

When this is useful

This is useful when you need to:

  • Show a design revision to a client or team
  • Document a bug fix or improvement
  • Compare app versions or feature changes
  • Create visual proof of progress

How it works

  1. Choose the before/after comparison layout
  2. Upload your before and after screenshots
  3. Export the comparison image

The result is a clean, balanced comparison that shows exactly what changed. No guessing, no explaining, just a clear visual difference that speaks for itself.
